### 🔭 I do research on variable stars

I work with **OGLE** survey data and my own observations to study how stars
change over time.

- **[SRV_period_evolution](https://github.com/kkolsut/SRV_period_evolution)** —
  measuring long-term period and coherence evolution of Galactic bulge
  long-period variables across the OGLE-II / III / IV baseline.
- **[VariableStarAnomalyDetection](https://github.com/kkolsut/VariableStarAnomalyDetection)** —
  unsupervised outlier detection for OGLE-IV RR Lyrae light curves, aiming at a
  ranked catalog of candidates worth following up (IsolationForest + UMAP + HDBSCAN).
- **[politropy](https://github.com/kkolsut/politropy)** — polytropic models of
  stars by numerically solving the Lane–Emden equation (in C).

### 📷 I build astrophotography tools

Software to take my own data from the telescope all the way to a light curve.

- **[picer](https://github.com/kkolsut/picer)** — a capture app for Canon EOS
  cameras: USB control, RAW → FITS conversion with full astronomical headers,
  live PSF preview, and a REST API. Runs headless on a Raspberry Pi at the scope.
- **[stanal](https://github.com/kkolsut/stanal)** — a DSLR photometry &
  variable-star analysis pipeline: calibrated FITS, aperture photometry,
  light curves, period finding, and classification.
